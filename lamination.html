Lamination, Vacuum Forming and CNC Milling

Start skating they said, it'll be fun they said

Our first project was to make our own skateboard. Lots of people use their skateboard to "walk" around campus so I thought it would be fun to have my own skateboard. To make this, I used seven sheets of thin wood, glued them together, and put them on a bent foam. I then put the foam with the board into a plastic bag and vacuumed the bag. The vacuumming process gave the board a bent shape. I laser cutted "passive aggressive" on the board. However, I should've laser cutted BEFORE I glued the board and bent it. The laser cutting process ended up tearing apart the edge of the wood sheets. I used a wood clamp to repair the skateboard. This was not one of the most successful projects this year;( However, it was my first experience working with wood and it was very fun!
