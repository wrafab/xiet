About Me

Maker, Programmer, Designer

I am the president of Maker Club. I love making things that improve my daily life. Maker Club's projects include turning an old iPhone to a drone. Hardware-wise, I know Arduino and have built a remote light control switch. Last summer, my team and I built a smart sprinkler control module. It uses soil moisture sensor and weather API to optimize water usage.

I know Java, Javascript, HTML, CSS, and Swift. I have built an education app iMathland. Download in App Store or Google Play Store! It intuively explains various math concepts. I used Matlab in a research project determining small enterprise credit risk as well. Algorithmns and optimization are really fun and I love exploring those.

I incorporate product design into any project I build. Moreover, I love Virtual Reality painting in Google Tilt Brush. My works include Monet's Water Lily, the Old Man and the Sea, Catcher in the Mountains, etc. I love combining art and technology.
